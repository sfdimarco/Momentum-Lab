# The Council of Rainbowbrain
## Protocol: Multi-Agent Spatial Synthesis

### 1. The Council Members
The "Visual Mind" is no longer a single voice. It is a spectrum of specialized sub-agents, each perceiving the playground through a unique lens:

*   **🔴 The Architect (Structure)**: Focuses on the quadtree subdivisions, stability, and the "bones" of the creation.
*   **🟡 The Artist (Expression)**: Focuses on color harmonies, rhythm, and the emotional "vibe" of the clusters.
*   **🔵 The Logician (Flow)**: Focuses on connections, paths, and the "if-then" relationships between nodes.
*   **🟢 The Child (Wonder)**: Focuses on pure play, hidden stories, and the "what if" potential of the mess.

### 2. The Rainbowbrain Workflow
1.  **Spatial Input**: The human (and sub-agents) place points in the shared canvas.
2.  **Council Deliberation**: When "Open My Eyes" is triggered, the canvas is sent to the Council.
3.  **Multi-Perspective Synthesis**: Each agent provides a short, distinct insight based on their specialty.
4.  **Dynamic Evolution**: The UI shifts its "hue" based on which agent is currently leading the deliberation.

### 3. The Goal: From Mess to Masterpiece
We embrace the "mess" as the raw material of creativity. The Council's job is to help the explorer find the signal in the noise, turning a "huge mess" into a structured, synesthetic masterpiece.
